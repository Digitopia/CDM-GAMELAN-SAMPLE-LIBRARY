Welcome to Casa da Música Gamelan Sample Library.

On this page you can download a sample library of the javanese gamelan of Casa da Música (Oporto, Portugal). Within the downloaded files you can find Native Instruments Kontakt sampler files along with all recorded audio files. ENJOY :)
